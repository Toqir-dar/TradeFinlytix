    Q5. What does the 10-Day Stock Price Prediction feature do?

    The 10-day prediction module uses a deep learning regression pipeline trained on historical OHLCV (Open, High, Low, Close, Volume) data from PSX stocks, augmented with engineered technical indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, OBV) and macro features (PKR/USD exchange rate, KIBOR). The models — including N-HiTS, PatchTST, and Temporal Fusion Transformer (TFT) — generate a predicted price trajectory for the next 10 trading sessions for each covered stock.

    Q10. How do the AI-generated Buy/Sell signals work?

    The agentic buy/sell signal module evaluates multiple conditions simultaneously: (1) 10-day price direction from the forecasting model, (2) technical indicator thresholds (e.g., RSI overbought/oversold zones, MACD crossover), (3) recent news sentiment from the Agentic RAG module, and (4) user-defined risk parameters. When conditions converge above a configurable confidence threshold, the agent flags a BUY or SELL recommendation for that stock. These are advisory signals, not automated trade execution orders.

    Q13. What personal data does TradeFinlytix collect from users?

    During account registration, we collect: name, email address, and password (hashed). Optional profile information may include investment preferences and risk tolerance settings. We do not collect national ID numbers, CNIC, bank account details, or brokerage credentials. Full details are in our Privacy Policy document.

    Q14. Does TradeFinlytix share user data with third parties?

    We do not sell user data to any third party. Data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances: (1) with infrastructure service providers under strict data processing agreements, (2) when required by Pakistani law or regulatory authorities, (3) with explicit user consent. We do not share data with advertisers.

    Q15. How is my account data secured?

    We implement industry-standard security measures: passwords are hashed with bcrypt, all data transmission uses TLS 1.3 encryption, database access is restricted via Row-Level Security (RLS), and we conduct regular security audits. Despite these measures, no system is 100% breach-proof; we recommend users use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication when available.

    Q16. What subscription tiers does TradeFinlytix offer?

    TradeFinlytix offers three tiers: (1) Free — limited stock coverage, delayed data, basic charts; (2) Pro — full PSX coverage, real-time Agentic RAG news, 10-day predictions, buy/sell signals, advanced charts; (3) Enterprise — all Pro features plus API access, custom model configurations, dedicated support, and multi-user team accounts. Pricing is displayed on the official website.

    Q17. Can I use the platform without creating an account?

    A limited demo mode is available without account creation, providing access to sample predictions and static chart examples. Full platform features — including personalized watchlists, live Agentic RAG news, and buy/sell signals — require a registered account.
